Early Childhood Music

Rubber Duckies is a class for children ages 2-3. Parents attend with their child in this early music class combining singing, movement and rhythm skills.
Materials provided, including rhythm sticks, books, and a rubber ducky! Each week presents songs, dances, games, reading, writing and playing that children can bring home.
  • ages 2-3
  • 6 week class
  • Cost: $100 + $25 enrollment fee (non-refundable)
